How to use crypto wallets

Types of Wallets

Hot Wallets(online)

Hot wallets are software-based crypto wallets connected to the internet, designed for frequent trading and daily use.

Cold Wallets

Cold wallets are physical, offline devices—often USB-like hardware—used for secure, long-term storage

Hot wallets offer high convenience but lower security, while cold wallets provide maximum security against online hacks
