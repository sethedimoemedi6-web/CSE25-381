Introduction to DE-FI

Defi stands for Decentralized financeit is like traditional banking(lending,borrowing trading,saving) but without banks or middleman

  • Anyone in the world with internet can use it (no permission needed)
  • You keep full control of your money
  • Everything is transparent you can see any transaction
  • it works 24/7 no holiday

De-fi use cases

  1. Lending and borrowing you can lend your crypto and earn interest or borrow money without paperworkexample:aave and compound
  2. Decentralized ExchangesSwap one crypto for another directly from your wallet
  3. Yield farming and Stakingputting your crypto in a pool and earn rewards,sometimes 5-50% a year
  4. Stablecoins Crypto money that stays around $1 (usdt,usdc,Dai).Great for saving and sending money cheaply
  5. InsuranceBuy protection against hacks or price crashes (example Nexus mutual)
  6. Derivitives and OptionsBet on price movements without actually earnint the coin (more advanced)

Smart contracts and Dapps

Smart contracts are like digital agreements that run by themselves on the blockchain

Think of them as vending machine:you put money in->w=the machine automatically gives you what you asked for. No person is needed

They are written in code (usually solidity on Ethereum) and once uploaded, they cannot be changed or stopped

Dapps (Decentralized Apps)are normal looking apps and websites that are powered by smart contrats

  • They run on the blockchin instead of a company server and no one can shut them down
  • You connet your wallet (metamask) and interact directly

Real Examples:

  • Uniswap = a Dapp that uses smart contracts to let you swap tokens
  • Aave = a Daap that uses smart contracts for lending and borrowing

How does Dao work

Everything is controlled by smart contracts and voting

How a Dao actually works(step by step):

  1. Members earn or buy a governence tokens(Example:uni for Uniswap DAO)
  2. Anyone with token can create proposal (e.g lets add a new feature or lets spend treasury money)
  3. All token holders vote on the proposal
  4. If the vote passes the smart contract automatically makes it happen no one can stop it

    5. Popular Daos

    • Uniswap Dao-runs the biggest decentralized exchange
    • Maker Dao- controls the Dai stablecoin
    • Gitcoin Dao- funds open source projects

    DAOs are the governance layer of DEFI.They let the community decide the future of the project instead of a single company
