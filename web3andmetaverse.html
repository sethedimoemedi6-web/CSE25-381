A begginers guide to metaverse

The metaverse is the next version of the internet.Instead of looking a flat websites and apps on the screen you can enter a 3d virtual world where you interact with other people in real time

Think of it as a mix between the internet video games and virtual reality. In the metaverse you can:

  • Meet friends and attend events
  • Buy sell and own virtual land and items
  • Play games,go to concerts, or even work
  • Shop with cryptocurrencies and NFTs

Popular examples include Decentraland,the sandbox,Roblox and fortnite

Understanding NFTs

NFT stands for Non Fungible TokensNon-Fungible means it is unique and cannot be replaced by something else

Unlike bitcoin or Usdt which are interchangeble like normal money every nft is one of a kind just like a real painting or a concert

NFT are digital certificates of ownership stored on the blockchain. They represent:

  • Digital art and collectibles (e.g. cryptopunks, Bored ape yacht club)
  • Virtual land and buildings in the metaverse
  • In-game items , characters and weapons
  • Music ,Videos and even real world assets

    • Because they are on the blockchain you can prove you own the asset and even resell it easily

GameFi

Gamefi means Game+Finance also known as play to earn P2E

It is blockchain gaming where you can earn real cryptocurrency or NFTs just by playing the game

In traditional game the items you buy or earn belong to the game company.In Gamefi,your items are NFTs that you truly own or you can sell them or use them in other games

How Gamefi works:

  • Play the game and complete Quests, battles or challenges
  • Earn cryptocurrency tokens as rewards
  • Own valuable in-game items as NFTs
  • Trade or sell your items on marketplaces

Popular Gamefi examples:Axie Infinity,The Sandbox,Decentraland and Gods unchained
