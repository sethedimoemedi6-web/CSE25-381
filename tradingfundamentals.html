What is trading and investing

InvestingIs buying and Holding Cryptocurrencies for a long time Months or years.The goal is to benefit from the overall growth of the project

Tradingis buying and selling cryptocurrencies in shorter time frame (days,weeks, or even hours and minutes ) to make profit from price changes

Think of it this way:

  • Investing = Buying a good project and growing with it
  • Trading = Trying to buy low and sell high quickly

What are fundamental and Technical Analysis

There are the two main ways to understand the crypto market

Fundamental Analysis (fA)
This means studying the real value and potential of a cryptocurrency.You look at:

  • The team behind the project
  • The technology and its usefullness
  • Adoption and real world use cases
  • Partnerships and community support
  • Token Economics (supply,utility)

Technical Analysis (TA)
This focuses on price charts and market data.Traders look at:

  • Price patterns and trends
  • Trading Volumes
  • Chart Indicators (Moving averages and RSi)
  • Support and resistence levels

TA helps predict short-term price movements based on past behaviour

Understanding market cycles and trends

The crypto market moves in cycles it does not go straight up or down forever

Main Market Cycles:

  • Accumulation Phase:Prices are low and reletively stable.Smart investors start buying
  • Bull Market(uptrend):Prices rise strongly.optimism is high
  • Distribution Phase:Early buyers start selling to lock in the profits
  • Bear Market (Downtrend):Price moving in range without clear direction

Trends are the general direction of the market:

  • Uptrend:prices making higher highs and higher lows
  • Downtrend:prices making lower highs and lower lows
  • Sideways:Price moving in a range without clear direction

How does pyschology affect the market

Common emotions in the market

  • Greed:People buy more when prices rise fast (FOMO - fear of missing out)
  • FEAR:People panic sell when prices drop quickly
  • Herd MEntality:Following what everyone is doing instead of thinking idependently

Understanding psychology helps you avoid making emotional decisions. successful traders have a clear plan and stick to it even when the market is very exciting or scary

Remember the saying :"Be fearful when others are greed and greed when others are fearful"-Warren Buffet
